Executive Profiles

Our Executives

Robert Thompson

Chairman Of The Board, President/ Chief Executive Officer

Robert is a proud native of Boston and a graduate of Johnson & Wales University, North Miami with a Associate of Science in Criminal Justice and Restaurant Management. Throughout his career, Robert has dedicated his experience and skill set to revamping the contracted security and investigations industry. Robert began working in the security industry as a security officer in 2003. Developing, and implementing improvements for the logistical and operational inadequacy at many client sites to which he was assigned lead to his promotion to Account Manager for a Industry powerhouse. After helping grow the company and streamlining its processes, Robert left the agency to pursue opportunities in a greater capacity, gaining valuable experience conducting threat assessments, varied types of security operations and complex investigations. His credentials also include Private Investigator, board certification as a “Certified Protection Professional” by ASIS. Robert is the Chairman Of The Board and President and Chief Executive Officer of Primetime Protective Services, specializing in high-end, customer essential security, investigation, training and consulting services. Robert can still be found in the field managing complex investigations and large-scale events when he isn’t busy conducting security assessments, drafting security plans, conducting training programs, or managing the day-to-day business of his security enterprise. In his free time, he spends a lot of time in his restaurant in the West End of Boston; he also enjoys spending time with his children, family, and close circle of friends, enjoys traveling, firearms training, and exploring other streams of income.


David Chaves

Board Member, Chief Operating Officer, General Counsel

David Chaves is responsible for the overall operations and business development of Primetime Protective Services, with a full spectrum of services: Armed/Unarmed security services including mobile patrol, executive protection as well as consulting investigative services and more. As the Chief Operating Officer, David has had success in creating meaningful relationships with law enforcement agencies and clients nationwide. His original mission of the company was to create an alliance with all local and state law enforcement agencies with emphasis on providing unparalleled professional security services with a wide range of service capabilities to clients. This standard has proven to be a successful and desired model since joining Primetime Protective Services in 2018. Prior to joining PPS, he was a senior FBI Official/Supervisory Special Agent assigned to the New York Division for twenty years. He served as the securities fraud program manager for the most visible securities cases prosecuted over the last decade including Bernie Madoff, Martha Stewart, Martin Shkreli and many more. He is widely recognized as the chief strategist in coordinating these complex white-collar investigations and for infiltrating corrupt participants in the hedge fund industry through the use of sophisticated techniques, undercover operations, and wire taps. He is a securities fraud subject matter expert and lectures to audiences around the world on securities fraud, Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA), compliance/regulation, best practices, leadership, and cyber threats to the financial industry. David has led delegations in foreign countries on behalf of FBI and State Department to assist judges and prosecutors on “rule of law” and protection of fundamental human rights. Prior to joining the FBI, he became the youngest Detective Sergeant in the State of Massachusetts and the first policeman to become an attorney and member of the Massachusetts Bar. He had a civil litigation practice in MA. David is regularly sought by print, online, and television media for his unique insight on a broad spectrum of topics. As an FBI Agent, he also worked closely with financial industry executives and legal teams to foster a culture of compliance, leadership, and responsibility.

George Rainey

Board Member, Vice President Of Operations

George Rainey is Primetime Protective Services Vice President of Operations. Mr. Rainey is in charge with overseeing the smooth operation of PPS’s business while keeping the company compliant with applicable rules and regulations, including those relating to export, government contracting, immigration, labor and employment and more. George also serves as a member of Primetime Protective Services Board of Directors. Mr. Rainey has been at PPS for fifteen (15) years. Prior to joining PPS, Mr. Rainey worked at New World Security Associates. George Rainey is also Vice President of Training & Professional Services for Primetime Protective Services. PPS provides Commercial Security, Retail Security, Residential, Corporate & Government Security, Executive Protection, Special Event Security Services and much more to customers nationwide. George is responsible for high-quality, efficient and repeatable delivery of services, as well as the innovation and technical development for advanced service offerings to the customer. Rainey has more than 15 years of Security Experience on a Management Level. During his tenure he has Managed and Developed over 500 Officers. Rainey possesses an undergraduate degree in Criminal Justice from Bunker Hill Community College. Rainey, who is also a realator, received his realtors' license in 2010 and volunteers with various organizations on the development of their security plans and assessment.
