Primetime Protective Services Employees

At PPS, we pride ourselves on "Providing Unparalleled Security Services." That is the attitude and willingness that we look for in our prospective employees, and that is what we recognize and reward in our active employees. We treat each of our employees with the utmost respect.That is why we engage directly with each of our employees to ensure the highest levels of communication, employee effectiveness and employee satisfaction. Primetime Protective Services employees are committed and engaged. We recognize the intangibles that each one of our employees has, and we create a work environment where they can utilize those intangibles on a daily basis.

Hiring and Placement

Our hiring and placement process is carefully designed to evaluate your individual strengths and to place you where you will be most effective as an employee of PPS.Do you love meeting new people and interacting with great individuals each and every day? Do you enjoy a quiet Corporate setting? Do you love the ability to multitask all while remaining alert and ready for any possible security threat? Or do you simply prefer focusing on one task at a time? We can guarantee you this, that no matter what answers you give to these questions. We will assure you that you are placed in a position where your never a liability but truly an asset to our company and valued clients.
Veterans are highly encouraged to apply.

Certification and Training

At PPS, we want you to be completely knowledgeable of exactly whats expected of you so that we get the best results. To assure that, our entire staff of Executives, Management personnel, Supervisors and Public Safety Officers receive prior site training, on-the-job, and on-going training with all Post Orders clearly written and reviewed by our Legal Team. We also offer state training and licensing fee assistance to all of our new officers.

Applicants with Disabilities

Primetime Protective Services is fully committed to ensuring our application process provides an equal employment opportunity to all job-seekers, including individuals with disabilities. If you believe you require a reasonable accommodation in order to apply for a job opening or to submit a cover letter and resume please contact us at HR@primetimeprotectiveservices.org. A Human Resource Specialist will assist any and all persons with a disability throughout the entire hiring process.
