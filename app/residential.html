Residential Security

Residential security services are a primary element of Primetime Protect​ive Services offered services. We provide security to a wide range of residential communities including gated communities, open-access communities, apartment complexes, and high-rise residential buildings. While some clients require a elevated level of security visibility, others may prefer a much lower security profile. Primetime Protective Services Account Managers work closely with property management companies, property owners, and boards of directors to determine each client’s security needs.

Protecting the Entry points to your community

A protected community produces a safe community. You want to keep your residents, their families and their guests safe, and a key part of maintaining their safety and keeping your neighborhood a desirable place to inhabit lies with your residential Public Safety Officers. At Primetime Protective Services, we possess expertise providing responsive, residential Public Safety Officers who truly become part of your community.

We are the Eyes and Ears of Your Community

We know Public Safety Officers serve as the initial and final impression of your community, complex or building. When matching Public Safety Officers to residential communities, we provide officers who are:

  • Friendly and enjoy working with the public
  • Attentive to detail so they can carry out the important tasks of their posts and patrols
  • Easy to communicate with so they can deliver a high level of customer service
